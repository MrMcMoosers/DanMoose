Welcome to the Test Randomizer/Grader!

Simply type in a question in the question box, its answer in the answer box, and click the 'Add' button. Do this for every question you want on the test.
When you have finished adding all the questions and would like to randomize the order, click the 'Randomize' button.
To grade the test after all the questions have been answered, click the 'Grade' button.
